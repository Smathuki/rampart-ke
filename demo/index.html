rampart-ke

Kenya-localized PII redaction that runs entirely in your browser.

🔒 On-device. Detection runs locally via WebAssembly/WebGPU. The model weights download once (~15 MB) then cache — after that you can disconnect Wi-Fi and redaction still works. No text is sent anywhere.
